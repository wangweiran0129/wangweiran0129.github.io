My Research Interests

My research interest, in one single sentence, is the design and optimization of networked system (especially for the low-latency network). Specifically, it straddles the complex domain of networked systems and its interdisciplinary interfaces, include but not limited to:
  • Network resource allocation and deployment
  • The research on Network Calculus itself and its application in the design of performance guaranteed networked systems
  • The application of machine learning and game theory in the optimization of networked systems
  • Telecom areas including 5G RAN and Cloud RAN (Now, I am working on the virtualized digital unit in uplink control plane)
